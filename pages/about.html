Our Story

Founded with the vision of preserving Maasai artistry, The Native is a community-centered initiative that empowers local artisans. We believe in sharing Maasai culture with the world while providing fair wages to our talented craftsmen and women.

Our Mission & Values

We are dedicated to sustainability, fair trade, and cultural preservation. By shopping with us, you are supporting a brighter future for our artisans and the Maasai community. We uphold ethical practices that honor the rich heritage of Maasai craftsmanship.

Our Impact

Every purchase helps provide essential income for Maasai families, supports local schools, and contributes to the preservation of Maasai culture. Together, we are making a difference, empowering artisans, and keeping traditions alive.
